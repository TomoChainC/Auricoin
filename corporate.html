FORFAITING

High rewards demand high risks, but with our forfaiting services, you can minimize the risks involved in high value international business transactions.  This will allow you to receive the payment immediately for accounts receivable in the medium term with a discount, without recourse to payment obligations.
AURICOIN, as a forfaiter, acts as an intermediate entity that buys accounts receivable.  The importer will have to pay the forfaiter to claim the goods.  This ensures a faster cash flow for its purpose, while eliminating the risk of default by the importer.
